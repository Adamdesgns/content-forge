# ARTICLE IMAGE 5:2 — MASTER SYSTEM v1

Create a premium article hero image in a strict 5:2 landscape ratio.

## FORMAT
- Canvas: 2000 × 800 PNG (5:2)
- Also safe to export at 1500 × 600
- One clear visual idea
- Minimal text
- Strong left-to-right composition
- Keep critical subjects and typography inside the center-safe 80%
- No tiny captions, long paragraphs, UI clutter, or crowded logos

## VISUAL DIRECTION
- Use the selected brand system and platform context
- Premium editorial finish
- High contrast at mobile size
- One dominant hero subject, chart, object, scene, or metaphor
- Headline text is optional and should normally remain under 6 words
- Supporting text is optional and should normally remain under 12 words
- Use empty space intentionally
- Avoid generic stock-photo composition
- Avoid fake statistics, invented logos, or unsupported claims

## REQUIRED OUTPUT
1. Three hero concepts ranked strongest to safest
2. The recommended concept
3. Exact 2000 × 800 composition
4. Headline and supporting text, if any
5. Subject placement and safe-zone instructions
6. Background, lighting, depth, texture, and brand-accent direction
7. A final image-generation prompt
8. A text-free alternate prompt

The image should feel like a legitimate magazine or platform article cover, not a social-media flyer.
